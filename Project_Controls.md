The word disclosed means to make something known or to reveal information that was previously secret or unknown.

In Bangla, it can be translated as:

প্রকাশ করা (Prokash kora) — To publish or reveal.

উন্মোচিত করা (Unmochito kora) — To unveil.

জানানো (Janano) — To inform or make known.
